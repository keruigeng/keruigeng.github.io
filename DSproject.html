Project Goals¶

The team intends to predict the consumers' other buying behavior based on their one-time purchasing information. In economics, some products are complementary, which means that consumer gains more utilities if they consume the two products together. However, whether the products are complementary or not is largely degermed by consumers preference. Through consumers purchasing behavior, their preference can be revealed. If we know more about consumers preference, we can recommend the corresponding product to them. This is an efficient way to promote selling.

Extraction, Transform, and Load (ETL)¶

Data and Table The data is from data.world, the enterprise data catalog for the modern data stack. This open and free platform provides rich data to public. I download the Fashion products on Amazon.com from this platform (https://data.world/promptcloud/fashion-products-on-amazon-com). Fashion products on Amazon.com is a pre-crawled dataset, taken as subset of a larger dataset (more than 7 million fashion products) that was created by extracting data from Amazon. This dataset contains consumer level purchasing information, including the item price, category, manufacturer, number available in stock, number of reviews and questions, et al. Most importantly, it tracks the consumers other purchasing behavior after reviewing or buying this item. This can help predict consumers purchasing behavior based on their existing buying information.

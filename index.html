Project Goals¶

The team intends to predict the consumers' other buying behavior based on their one-time purchasing information. That's said,how many items a consumer will buy after buying the certain product, such as the apple computer. In economics, some products are complementary, which means that consumer gains more utilities if they consume the several products together. In marketing, good shopping experience would boost consumers' trust towards seller, thus stimulating more purchasing. If we know which features of a certain product can stimulate more purchasing, Amason can recommend the corresponding product to comsumers so that comsumers will buy more products in total. This is an efficient way to promote selling.

Extraction, Transform, and Load (ETL)¶

Data and Table The data is downloaded from data.world, the enterprise data catalog for the modern data stack. This open and free platform provides rich data to public. I download the Fashion products on Amazon.com from this platform (https://data.world/promptcloud/fashion-products-on-amazon-com). Fashion products on Amazon.com is a pre-crawled dataset, taken as subset of a larger dataset (more than 7 million fashion products) that was created by extracting data from Amazon. This dataset contains consumer level purchasing information, including the item price, category, manufacturer, number available in stock, number of reviews and questions, et al. Most importantly, it tracks the consumers other purchasing behavior after reviewing or buying this item. This data is extreamely helpful for my project question. It contains rich set of product information for every purchase. Also it tracks this consumer's other purchasing behavior after buying this product. This can help predict consumers other purchasing behavior based on their existing buying information.
